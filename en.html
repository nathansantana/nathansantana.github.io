Piramix

Piramix it's a project developed by Hakuna group during the first period of Fatec's digital games course in Americana/SP. The proposal was to develop a child analog game. The project seeks to exercise psychomotor, creativity and cognition in kids from 10 years old.

The game is a team competition, each one needs to accomplish chalenges (Draw, Dices, Words and gestures) and earn pieces. The goal is to build a pyramid.

Huizinga Prize 2019 (1º semestre)
The best projects receive a certificate.
Hakuna Group: Nathan Santana, Lana Schimitez, Evaldo Araújo, Marcelo Danelon and Rafael Lee.
